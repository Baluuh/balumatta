About Me

I'm Balu Matta, a Graphic and UI/UX Designer with a passion for creating seamless, user-centered digital experiences. My work blends product design, UX research, and strategy to deliver intuitive and responsive web designs that resonate with users.

With experience in various design disciplines, I've developed expertise in creating visually engaging and functional designs using tools like Figma, Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, XD, Premiere Pro, After Effects, and Canva. My proficiency in HTML and CSS allows me to seamlessly integrate design with Frontend development, ensuring a cohesive and responsive user experience.

I've also worked as a content creator at Pixeltest, a startup in Hyderabad, where I contributed remotely to various content creation projects. This role enhanced my ability to communicate complex ideas in a clear, concise manner and sharpened my skills in social media marketing. Outside of work, I enjoy exploring new creative avenues and relaxing with a good movie or discovering new culinary experiences.
